Alex Wheeler
User Experience Designer

Hi, I'm Alex Wheeler

I am a professional User Experience Designer based in San Francisco, California. I have a professional background in interaction design and front end development, and a Bachelors of Science in Design from the University of Cincinnati.

While each step in the design thinking process is vital, I believe empathy is the key to great UX.

I love all design.

But some things I particularly enjoy include...

UX Design

User Experience Design is all about understanding your users so you can solve meaningful problems.

UI Design

In a world where every pixel is pushed; beautiful, intuitive, engaging user interfaces are more important than ever.

Product Design

Fail early, fail often. Taking a product concept, iterating on it, prototyping it and getting it infront of users early is the heart of product design.

Web Development

From websites to prototypes, I've been known to dabble in the ever changing world of web development. HTML5, CSS3, and Javascript are my tools of choice.
